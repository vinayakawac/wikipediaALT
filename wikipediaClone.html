The Free Encyclopedia

From today's featured article

Wikipedia is a free online encyclopedia that anyone can edit. It was launched on January 15, 2001, by Jimmy Wales and Larry Sanger. Wikipedia has grown to become one of the most visited websites on the internet, with millions of articles in hundreds of languages. The content is written collaboratively by volunteers around the world, and almost all of its articles can be edited by anyone with access to the site. Wikipedia's articles cover an enormous range of topics and are freely available to anyone with an internet connection.

Did you know ...

  • ... that the name "Wikipedia" is a portmanteau of "wiki" and "encyclopedia"?
